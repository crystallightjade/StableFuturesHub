Local Resources in San Diego

Welcome to the Local Resources page! Below is a list of organizations and programs available in San Diego that provide support, services, and resources to the community:

Health Resources

  • San Diego Health and Human Services Agency - Provides access to health services and resources for individuals and families.
  • Planned Parenthood of the Pacific Southwest - Offers reproductive health and educational services.

Food Assistance

  • San Diego Food Bank - Distributes food and provides hunger relief to those in need.
  • Feeding America - Works with local food pantries and provides emergency food assistance.

Housing Support

  • San Diego Housing Commission - Offers various programs to assist with affordable housing.
  • Father Joe's Villages - Provides housing and supportive services for individuals experiencing homelessness.

Employment Services

  • San Diego Workforce Partnership - Provides job training and workforce development services.
  • Career Center - Offers career counseling and job search assistance.

Education and Youth Services

  • San Diego Public Library - Offers educational programs and resources for all ages.
  • Big Brothers Big Sisters of San Diego - Provides mentoring programs for youth.

For further information, please reach out to these organizations directly or visit their respective websites.
