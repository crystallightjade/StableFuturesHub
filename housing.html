Step-by-step: how to apply for foster-youth transitional housing in CA

  1. Ask your team: connect with your County Housing Coordinator / ILP (Independent Living Program) coordinator.
  2. Also ask: your social worker, CASA, or attorney can help you start the process.
  3. Use this official CA overview: CDSS Transitional Housing Programs
  4. When you call programs: ask what documents they need (ID, foster status verification, school/work plan, etc.).

This is the official CA Department of Social Services guidance for THP programs and how to get started.

"What do I say when I call?" (script)

Hi, my name is _____. I'm a current/former foster youth in San Diego County. I'm looking for transitional housing and support. Can you tell me: (1) eligibility, (2) openings/waitlist, (3) required documents, and (4) next steps?

Note on Rise & Thrive / Better Chances

Rise & Thrive has local news coverage as an emerging nonprofit; confirm their official intake process directly. Better Chances appears listed outside San Diego County — verify fit before relying on it.
