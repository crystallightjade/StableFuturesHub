San Diego-Specific Resources

Emergency Services

If you are in immediate danger or need urgent help, please call 911.

Mental Health Resources

  • San Diego Access and Crisis Line: Call (888) 724-7240 or text 'HOME' to 741741 to reach a trained counselor.
  • NAMI San Diego: Offers support and education for individuals and families affected by mental illness. Call (619) 543-1434.
  • Mental Health America of San Diego County: Provides various services. Visit their website for more information.

Domestic Violence Resources

  • San Diego Domestic Violence Hotline: Call (888) 385-4657 for assistance and shelter options.
  • The Women's Resource Center: Offers support services and shelter for women and children. Call (760) 757-3500.

Substance Abuse Help

  • Alcoholics Anonymous San Diego: Offers support for those struggling with alcoholism. Visit the website for meeting locations.
  • San Diego Narcotics Anonymous: A support group for those struggling with drug addiction. Call (619) 647-6040.
